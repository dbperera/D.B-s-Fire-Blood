D.B's Fire & Blood

To Run:

1. Open FIT1048 Assignment 3 (Microsoft Visual Studio Solution File)
2. Run Local Windows Debugger
3. Wait for project to load symbols
4. Play
5. Enjoy!

How To Play:

D.B's Fire & Blood uses strategy based combat, mixing and matching physical and magical attacks to suit the 
situation. Commands are entered by typing P (Physical), M(Magical) or S(Special Move), then hitting enter. In
fights with more than one enemy, type a number to choose the enemy and press enter.
